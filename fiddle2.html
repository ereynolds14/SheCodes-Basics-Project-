All about Fiddle

and why it's different from violin

Fiddles and violins are the same instrument but the difference in the term refers to the type of music played on each. Violin is usually assocaited with a classical and jazz styles of playing whereas a fiddle is generally the word used for bluegrass, celtic and old time music to name a few genres. To summarize, the instruments are the same but the type of music and the manner in which they are played are different.
